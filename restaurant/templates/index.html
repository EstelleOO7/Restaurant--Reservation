About

Based in Quebec, Canada, Tribes and Taste is a family-owned restaurant, focused on traditional recipes served with a modern twist.

The chefs draw inspiration from Italian, Nigerian, Greek, and Turkish culture and have a menu of 12-15 items that they rotate seasonally.

The restaurant has a rustic and relaxed atmosphere with moderate prices, making it a popular place for a meal any time of the day.

Tribes and Taste is a family-owned restaurant, Damilola and Estelle, who moved to Canada to pursue their shared dream of owning a restaurant.

To craft the menu, they rely on family recipes and their experience as chefs in Italy and other countries.

Damilola does all the marketing for the restaurant and led the effort to expand the menu beyond classic Italian to incorporate additional cuisines from the Mediterranean region.
